About this tool

This calculator implements the R* Composite Priority Index, a rapid assessment methodology for evaluating climate data vulnerability developed through 70+ structured expert interviews across federal agencies, academic institutions, and international data centers.

  • R* framework: S.C. Diggs, T.R. Karl, R. Keeling — Ensemble Approach to Data Risk Assessment (v2.0)
  • RRS and DIRS formal instruments: Repository Crisis Scorecards project — doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.15208172 — based on Schuster, Mayernik, Mullendore & Marquis (2023), Bull. Amer. Meteor. Soc., 104, E2053–E2064. Contact: rcs@esipfed.org
  • Associated with: CO-DAN (Climate/Ocean Data Action Network) and ESIP
