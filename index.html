About this tool

This calculator implements the R* Composite Priority Index, a rapid assessment methodology for operational triage in climate data preservation. R* is designed for crisis conditions — it assumes institutional instability rather than steady-state operations, and explicitly accounts for the human expertise dimension of data risk. It is not a substitute for strategic value assessment (see Karl utility scoring); it provides the urgency signal that tells you where to look first.

  • R* framework: S.C. Diggs, T.R. Karl, R. Keeling — Ensemble Approach to Data Risk Assessment (v2.0). Developed through 70+ structured expert interviews and validated through application to NOAA, IRI, and NODD dataset inventories.
  • RRS formal instrument: Repository Crisis Scorecards project — doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.15208172 — based on Schuster, Mayernik, Mullendore & Marquis (2023), Bull. Amer. Meteor. Soc., 104, E2053–E2064. Contact: rcs@esipfed.org
  • Associated with: CO-DAN (Climate/Ocean Data Action Network) and ESIP
